About

This web page is a humbly devoted to the most interesting guy I've ever known, my grandpa
Naum Dorfman.

Naum was born in 1940 to a jewish family a year before 2nd ww stuck russia. One year old whose father had been drafted into the army, had to escape approaching nazi forces. Naum's mother Faina Dorfman took four children including my one year old grandpa and evacuated to eastern russia. During war his father served in the protecting forces near Leningrad, a famous historical blockade called "The Siege of Leningrad" for which he recieved an order of the red star for bravery. At the end of the war by some mircle my grandpa, his mother and two sisters who survived the war reunited with their father and relocated back to the city were my grandfather Naum was born - Polotsk.

His military service my grandpa undertook in a northen russian island called "Novaya Zemlya". Novaya Zemlya was the site of one of the two major nuclear test sites managed by the USSR, used for air drops and underground testing of the largest of Soviet nuclear bombs. Some stories from his serviece my grandpa keeps to himself up to this day, but he told me that he used to encounter white polar bears on a daily basis, and that the soldiers treated them like cats, even let them get close.

In 1964 my grandpa married my beautiful grandma who he loved deeply, but bacause his parents didn't accept her he had to relocate from his birth town and move to a place where he could find a job - a village near the city Krasnoyarsk, Siberia. There, under extreme coldness (I assume) my father has been born, In the middle of a forest!

My father and his little sister grew up in that same city their ancestors lived generations in - Polotsk, where their mad parents later returned. In 1991 my father moved to Israel, and in 1993 his parents and younger sister followed him. This day my cool grandpa is a cute old man who you'd never assume has such an interesting past. From a kid who survived the war and the following hungry years in russia, brave soldier who helped scientists research radioactive lands to a kind and carrying family man. I love my grandpa and I think he cooks the best cabbage soup in the world(russian soup called Shchi).

Web1 was the very beginning of the world wide web, it's main purpose was to present data. Whereas web2 braught us the integration, the ability to share information and learn from our peers. Web3 made things a bit smarter, turning the web to semantic web allowing information to link to each other tagging information automaticaly.



Differences of web 1.0 web 2.0 and Web 3.0

Web 1.0 was all about static content, one way publishing of content without any real interaction between readers or publishers or each other.

Web 2.0 is more about 2 way communication through social networking, blogging, wikis, tagging, user generated content and video.

Web 3.0 is curiusly undefined. AI and the web learning what you want and delivering you a Personalized web experience.

Web 1.0 is the first generation of web and was created in 1989 by Tim Berners-Lee

Learning with an example

Say my grandpa asked me to buy him a swiss army knife and I would put a query "swiss army knife" in the search engine

  • Web 1.0 would direct me to a static web page with information about swiss knifes and maybe a cell phone number so I could call a store that sells them

  • Web 2.0 would direct me into a dynamic web-store that would take my order and inform me about the shipping status as it changes

  • Web 3.0 would show me advertisements of swiss army knifes or maybe home tools or just knifes, while I browse other sites.
